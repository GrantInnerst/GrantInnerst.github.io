Bio

I currently hold the position of Assistant Professor in the Mathematics Department at Shippensburg University. I completed my M.S. and Ph.D. in the Department of Statistical Science from Baylor University under the direction of David Kahle.

Before Baylor, I completed my undergraduate work at Shippensburg University, where I earned a B.S. in Mathematics where I worked with Ben Galluzzo. Through Ben's mentorship and two wonderful workshops at SAMSI, I realized my interest in statistics, which started me down this crazy path.

In my free time, I love to spend time with my beautiful wife Melissa, who is also a statistician, and our two dogs Einstein and Newton. We love to travel, geocache, and go bowling.
