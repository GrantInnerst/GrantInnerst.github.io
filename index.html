About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Shippensburg University. I completed my M.S. and Ph.D. in Statistical Science from Baylor University under the direction of David Kahle. My research generally lies in the field of algebraic statistics. I am especially interested implementing algebraic statistical methods in R

Before Baylor, I completed my undergraduate work at Shippensburg University, where I earned a B.S. in Mathematics with a certification in Secondary Education.
